YOLOv8 Benchmark: How to Use
This is a visualization of results obtained by training 13 different YOLOv8 models for vehicle detection and testing them on 6 devices with several additional settings.
  1. Select a device and batch size closest to your interest.
  2. Deselect models (architectures, resolutions, quantization) which you want to inherently ignore.
  3. Investigate chart 1 (compare performances) and use the additional filter to further filter out unsuitable models.
  4. Use chart 2 to make sure the selected models perform well in all relevant use-cases.
  5. Use chart 3 to compare FPS of models on each device.
Remember that on chart 1 and 3, in the top right corner, you can see the precise values for the current cursor position.

To see precise mAP / FPS values, you can hover with your mouse on the corresponding data points (on rows in Additional Filter, data points in chart 1 and 2, and Y-axis labels in chart 3).
